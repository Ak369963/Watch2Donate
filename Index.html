Our Mission

KindView is a revolutionary platform that turns your everyday online activities into meaningful support for those in need. We believe that small actions, when multiplied by millions of people, can transform the world.

How It Works

This website is funded entirely by advertisements. The more you engage with the ads on our site, the more revenue we generate. We then donate 98.2% of that revenue to verified charities and organizations that provide food, clean water, education, and healthcare to people in need around the world.

We retain only 1.8% to cover our operational costs - server maintenance, development, and administrative expenses. This ensures that your engagement directly translates into real impact.

Transparency Promise

We are committed to complete transparency. Every quarter, we publish detailed reports showing exactly how much we've earned from ads and where every rupee has been donated. You can track the impact of your engagement in real-time.
